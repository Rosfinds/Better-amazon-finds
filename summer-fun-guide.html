🌿 A Final Word from My Heart

Summer has a way of passing by faster than we expect.

Life gets busy. There are chores to finish, meals to cook, work to do, appointments to keep, and endless things competing for our attention. It's easy to believe we need to plan something extraordinary to create meaningful memories.

But often, the moments our children treasure most are the simplest ones.

  • 🦋 A walk outside looking for butterflies
  • 🎲 A game around the kitchen table
  • 🏕️ A backyard adventure
  • 🎨 A craft made with tiny hands
  • 📖 A bedtime story read together

These ordinary moments become extraordinary because we were there with them.

Children may not remember every toy they received, but they will remember how loved they felt, how often we laughed together, and the time we chose to simply be present.

My prayer is that this guide inspires your family to slow down, enjoy one another, and make memories that will last far beyond this summer.

As followers of Christ, we are reminded that the greatest gift we can give our children isn't found in things—it is found in loving them, teaching them, and pointing them toward Jesus through the way we live each day.

"Train up a child in the way he should go, and when he is old he will not depart from it."
💚 Psalm 127:3
